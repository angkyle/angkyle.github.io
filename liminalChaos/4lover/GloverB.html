You step into the pool of acid, and you yell in pain as it burns your flesh.

"You foolish human. Please. You will kill yourself trying to save me."

The beast approaches closer, and you ready your blade with one hand while gripping Brenil's hand with the other.
As it charges to snap, you slice off its heads.

As you do, you pull with all your strength to get Brenil out, despite feeling the flesh of your arms falling off.
Both you and Brenil are bleeding gravely.

Just as the beasts' heads grow back, you lean Brenil over you and run as fast as you can back to the tribe.
