Another week passes.
Brenil seems to have gained some mobility back in their arms.
But they still can't walk.

There were more than a few nights when screams of pain can be heard from their hut
as the caretaking tribe member changes their bandages.

You walk in to visit them.

"I don't know how much longer I can deal with this pain." Brenil confesses.
"Sometimes, I'd rather die than endure another day."

You ask if you can help in any way.

"Just be with me."

You sit beside them, and hold their hand.
