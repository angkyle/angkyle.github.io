You're a few inches from the cave, but you start to feel dizzy from losing so much blood.
As the creature catches up while you slow, an arm reaches out from the cave and pulls you in.

The creature growls and hisses at the opening of the cave––only its snout fitting inside.

You sigh in relief, grasping your arm in pain.
You turn and see the faceless human who pulled you inside.
