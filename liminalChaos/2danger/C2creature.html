As you walk, the rash gets worse, and blood drips onto the ground.
You hear a growl behind you. You hope it isn't what you think it might be.

"What the--"

The creature stands there for a while, watching you intently, and its gaze follows the blood dripping down your arm.

You reach for one of the fruit you picked––the creature's eyes follow your hand.
You throw the fruit as hard and as far as you can. And with astonishing speed, the creature runs after it.

In haste, you run through the forest, with your heart pounding in your throat.
