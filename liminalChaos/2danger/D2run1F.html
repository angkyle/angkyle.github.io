You keep running despite the pain of the rough terrain against your shoe-less feet.
Despite your persistence, you lose your footing and trip. The creature comes even closer.
You hear whistling from a nearby tree.

"Hey! Over here! Come on."
It doesn't sound like a language you know, but you understand them.
A faceless human-like creature gestures you over to hide in a cave near them.
Just as the monstrous beast falls inches behind––you get up and miss its grasp by a second.
The faceless human grabs you by the arm and leads you to the cave. You follow them along, and both of you nimbly rush through the forest.
