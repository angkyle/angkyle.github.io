Just as you fall into a deep sleep, you wake to moisture dripping on your forehead.
"What the--"

You hold your breath. You feel panic engulf you.
The creature stands there for a while, hovering over you.
You reach for one of the fruit you picked––the creature's eyes follow your hand.

You throw the fruit as hard and as far as you can. And with astonishing speed, the creature runs after it.

In haste, you run out the cave, with your heart pounding in your throat.
