"Stay here. If you don't want me to take your blood,
I'll have to kill those predators myself and come back for you."
The faceless human leaves.

It gets dark. The faceless human comes back with blood all over their arms.
"Let's go. We have to go now." They say urgently.
You run with them through the night.
With every step, you weaken, and by the moon's peak hour, you black out.

You feel the faceless human nudging you awake,
but your body gives in.


After what seems like a few minutes, you wake up by a pink lake with the faceless human.
